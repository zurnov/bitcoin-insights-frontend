Privacy Policy

BTC Insights is designed with privacy in mind. We do not create accounts, track users, or sell data.

What We Do Not Collect

  • ✓No registration or login required — ever.
  • ✓We do not store the Bitcoin addresses or transaction hashes you search.
  • ✓We do not use advertising networks or tracking pixels.
  • ✓We do not set persistent first-party cookies.
  • ✓We do not sell, rent, or share any user data with third parties.

Server Logs

Like all web servers, our infrastructure automatically records standard access logs — IP address, request path, timestamp, and browser type (User-Agent). These logs are used solely for security monitoring and performance diagnostics. They are not shared with third parties and are rotated regularly. No search queries or blockchain identifiers are persisted in these logs.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third parties to deliver the service. Each operates under its own privacy policy:

Cloudflare

CDN and DDoS protection. Cloudflare terminates TLS and may log request metadata. See Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.

Google Fonts

Font delivery. A connection is made to Google's servers when the page loads. See Google's Privacy Policy.

Ko-fi

Optional donation link. Only applies if you click through to the Ko-fi page. See Ko-fi's Privacy Policy.

Bitcoin Blockchain Data

The Bitcoin addresses and transactions you look up on BTC Insights are public by design — they exist on the Bitcoin blockchain, which is a fully public, immutable ledger. Searching for an address or transaction on our site does not make that data "more public." We display what is already visible to anyone running a Bitcoin node.

Your Rights

Because we do not collect personal data, there is nothing to access, correct, or delete. If you believe we hold data about you, or if you have any privacy-related questions, contact us through the donate page.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated over time to reflect changes in the service or applicable law. Material changes will be noted with an updated date below.
